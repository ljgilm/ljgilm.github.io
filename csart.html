Computer Science & Art

Wormwhole

I collaborated with computer science students and worked as an art student to create our game Wormhole. I created and designed all the artwork for the game.

The objective of the game is to reach the back of the ship to repair it in order to escape the black hole. However, before you can do that you must complete tasks and fight anomalies which spawn in the location you die. The more you die the more anomalies you have to fight.
